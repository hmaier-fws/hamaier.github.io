mdEditor Announcements

Announcements regarding mdEditor updates and new features are posted to the "mdEditor-announce" group. To receive notifications of new postings, you can sign up to join the email list in two ways:

  1. Use or create Google account: Go to the mdEditor-announce group and click the "Sign in" button. On the sign in page, either enter your Google acccount information or select the "Create account" link.

  2. If you prefer not to use a Google account: Send an email to inquiry@mdeditor.org with the text "join" in the subject line. You will be administratively added to the mdeditor-announce distribution list.
